# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want this project to be useful, welcoming, and safe for contributors at all levels.

Examples of positive behavior:

- being respectful and constructive
- assuming good intent while giving honest technical feedback
- focusing on ideas, implementation, and outcomes rather than personal attacks
- helping newer contributors understand project conventions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or intimidation
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- bad-faith arguments or repeated derailing
- sharing private information without permission

## Maintainer Responsibility

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards, moderating discussions, and keeping collaboration productive.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code of conduct.

For serious concerns, contact the maintainer privately rather than escalating publicly.
